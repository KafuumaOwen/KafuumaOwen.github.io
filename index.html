"Try MediSave,Save time"

MediSave is a new mobile application that enables one to locate a nearby health centre according to their current location.
It also indicates the services offered at the centre.

Why MEDISAVE?

Medisave is a great way to manage your life.
MediSave is a user-friendly app designed to get feedback from the user about his/her experience at a particular facility. Through this feedback, health facilities can get to know what to improve in order to better the experience of their patients.
